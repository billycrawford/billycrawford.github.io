Marina

Marina Lambrini Diamandi is a singer-songwriter that performs under the name Marina and the Diamonds. Her start into fame started when she placed second in the BBC's Sound of 2010.

Her debut studio album, The Family Jewels entered the UK Albums Chard at number 5 and was certified gold by the British Phonographic Industry. Her follow up album Electra Heart became her first number-one project in the UK, where it was certified gold.

Marina describes herself as an "indie artist with pop goals." Her music incorporates indie pop, new wave and integrates elements of electropop.
